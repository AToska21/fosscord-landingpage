About Fosscord

Fosscord is free open source software compatible to discord. It is a selfhostable Chat, Voice and Video platform similar to Slack and Rocket.chat.

  • Discord-compatible
  • Selfhostable
  • Open Source
  • Configurable
  • Secure
  • Decentralized
  • Extendable
  • Themable

Fosscord is a free open source software compatible to discord. It is a selfhostable Chat, Voice and Video platform which is Discord-compatible. Fosscord is not sponsored, endorsed or administered by, or associated with Discord.
