About Fosscord

Fosscord is a FOSS (free open source software) chat, voice and video platform compatible with the Discord API.

Fosscord is
  • Discord-compatible
  • Selfhostable
  • Open Source
  • Configurable
  • Secure
  • Decentralized
  • Extendable
  • Themable

Fosscord is compatible to Discord's APIs. Fosscord is not sponsored, endorsed or administered by, or associated with Discord.
